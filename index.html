12:30pm
Alcatraz Island Tour
Don't skip the audio tour — narrated by former guards and inmates, it's one of the best audio experiences in the country. The cellhouse, solitary confinement, and escape attempt stories are gripping.
"The Rock is unlike anything you've experienced — and unlike anything you'll find anywhere else."
12:45pm
Lunch — Z & Y Restaurant, Chinatown
655 Jackson St, Chinatown — right on the way to North Beach. One of the best Chinese restaurants in SF. Sichuan cuisine, proper sit-down, locals eat here. Bold, memorable flavors. Walk in — reservations only for parties of 4+; call (415) 981-8988 for same-day.
2:00pm
North Beach → Filbert Steps
Start at Washington Square Park. Walk up Filbert Street to Telegraph Hill — steep but short, great views over the bay at the top. Watch for the famous wild parrots. Budget 90 relaxed minutes.
4:00pm
The Buena Vista
2765 Hyde St — 5 min walk from the hotel, right on the waterfront. Famous for Irish Coffee since 1952. A genuine SF institution and a perfect afternoon stop after a big day on the island.
4:30pm
Lombard Street (Optional)
Already saw it yesterday. Skip if tired — you've had a full day.

The Day's Plan
7:30am
Pick Up Rental Car
Pick up at San Francisco Fisherman's Wharf · Conf. 1595574001 — your wheels for the SF & Napa leg. Drive north on Hwy 101.
8:00am
Vista Point — Walk the Bridge
After crossing the Golden Gate heading north, stay far right and take the "Vista Point" exit immediately. Walk out on the pedestrian path from the Marin side — great straight-on bridge view. 15–20 min. If the lot is full, proceed directly to Battery Spencer.
8:20am
Battery Spencer
2 min drive from Vista Point via Conzelman Rd. The classic postcard shot — Golden Gate Bridge with SF skyline behind it. Best view in the Headlands. 20–25 min. The photo you'll actually frame.
9:00am
⚠ Reservation Required
Muir Woods
Book the 9:00–9:30am arrival window at recreation.gov — required and sells out. Main boardwalk + Bridge 2 loop only. Hard stop 10:30am — don't let it slip or Napa falls apart. Ancient coast redwoods — genuinely awe-inspiring.
10:30am
Depart for Napa
Don't add stops. ~90 min drive east into wine country.
12:15pm
Lunch — Gott's Roadside
Oxbow Public Market area. Fast, casual, genuinely good. Perfect reset before your first wine tasting.
2:30pm
✓ Booked · 2:30pm
Stag's Leap Wine Cellars
The winery that changed the wine world. In 1976 their Cab beat the finest French Bordeaux in a blind tasting by French experts — the legendary Judgement of Paris. $95pp.
"When the French judges learned what they had just scored highest, they were reportedly mortified. A four-year-old California winery had beaten wines considered the world standard for centuries."
— The 1976 Judgement of Paris
